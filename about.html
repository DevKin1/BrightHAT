About BrightHAT

Our Mission

We are on a mission to help people fulfill their learning needs from anywhere at anytime.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be globally recognized as an enabler of borderless outcome-focused learning.

Our People

Our team includes experienced educators and technologists passionate about using technology to improve learning outcomes for students and all learners.

Our Story

BrightHAT began as founders’ discussions to engage their children during COVID-19, evolving into brainstorming sessions that brought the idea to life.

The BrightHAT™ Way

BrightHAT’s way of teaching is inspired by Liz Kolb’s Triple E Framework, which is designed to foster engagement in learning goals, enhancement of learning goals, and extension of learning goals.

Our instructional strategies are technology driven to ensure that authentic engagement is occurring, while lessons are developed to help students stay focused as the instruction progresses. We use technology to support active learning, differentiation, personalization, higher-level thinking, and real-world connections in ways that traditional tools could not. Every instructor imbibes the BrightHAT way within days of joining our team.
